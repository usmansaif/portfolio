I developed and managed a robust eCommerce platform for Net Sites USA, a prominent car seat manufacturing business. The project encompassed end-to-end development and comprehensive maintenance for their official store and its associated network of marketing sites.

Some Features:
  • Order Management Streamlined processes for order placement, tracking, and fulfillment.
  • User Management Efficient handling of customer accounts, roles, and permissions.
  • Payment Management Integrated secure payment gateways for seamless transactions.
  • Bulk Inventory Updates Automated systems for managing large-scale inventory changes.
Sister Sites Management:
  • Oversaw 69 sister sites, specifically designed for marketing and localized content across different USA states.
  • Created new pages to expand the sites' reach and content.
  • Updated existing content to ensure accuracy, relevance, and consistency across all sites.
  • Conducted regular site maintenance to ensure top-notch performance and user experience.
Server and Hosting Management:
  • Managed all sites through WHM/cPanel (self hosted), ensuring efficient hosting and streamlined administrative operations.
  • Monitored and maintained the web server, ensuring high availability, performance, and security.
  • Performed server optimizations, backups, and troubleshooting to prevent downtime or disruptions.
