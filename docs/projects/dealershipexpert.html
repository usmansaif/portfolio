Dealership Experts is a specialized job portal designed exclusively for connecting automotive experts with dealerships and auto-related businesses. The platform streamlines the recruitment process by providing a dedicated space for skilled auto professionals, including mechanics, technicians, sales consultants, and service advisors, to find relevant opportunities in the automotive industry.

Key features of the portal include:
  • Job Listings: Comprehensive job postings from dealerships and auto companies, categorized by roles, skills, and locations.
  • Resume Management: Allows candidates to create, upload, and manage professional resumes tailored to automotive roles.
  • Search and Matching: Advanced search filters for matching candidates with the best job opportunities based on their experience and skills
  • Employer Dashboard: Enables dealerships to manage job postings, review applications, and streamline candidate hiring.
  • Candidate Dashboard: Helps job seekers track applications, save jobs, and receive notifications for matching job openings.
