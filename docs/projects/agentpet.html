agentpet.com

Online platform to buy and sell pets along with eCommerce capabilities to sell pet accessories and food. An easy tool to find nearby pet stores. Pet Owners also have the option to avail of some pet relocation services.

Features
  • Pet Accessories
  • Pet Food Store
  • Pet sale and purchase ads
  • Pet relocation
  • Find Nearby Pet Shops
  • Forum

Origin Lahore, Pakistan

  • Laravel
  • PHP
  • MYSQL
  • JQuery
  • Bootstrap
